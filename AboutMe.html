Ilana is a creative entertainment marketer, social media specialist and music/culture journalist. For over four years, she has worked in social media and marketing with brands on the client and agency sides including Qello Concerts, Crown Royal, SunTrust Bank, Campbell's Soup, Zappos, PHYTO, La Roche-Posay, ColorProof and more. Additionally, Ilana has moonlighted as a freelance journalist for five years for publications including The New York Times/T Magazine, Newsweek/The Daily Beast, Interview Magazine, NYLON Magazine, Paste Magazine, VICE Magazine, Baeblemusic, Complex Magazine, Refinery29 and Bullett Magazine. Most recently, she was the Marketing Manager at Qello Concerts. Prior to her career, Ilana attended Rutgers University where she received her B.A. in both English and Journalism & Media Studies.
